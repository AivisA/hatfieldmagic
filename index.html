Hatfield Magic

About

Hatfield Magic is an amateur basketball club located in Hatfield, Hertfordshire. Magic is currently playing in Herts Premier League Disvison 2. The practice sessions are run on Mondays at 8pm in Birchwood Leisure Centre.
